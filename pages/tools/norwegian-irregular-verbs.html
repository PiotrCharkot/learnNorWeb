Norwegian Irregular Verbs

120+ common irregular (strong) verbs in Norwegian Bokmål — with infinitive, present tense, past tense, and past participle. Searchable and grouped by vowel-change class.

📌 Bokmål forms only. These are the standard written Bokmål forms. Some verbs have alternative acceptable forms; the most common variant is listed. Irregular verbs are verbs that don't follow the regular -et/-te/-de pattern — they change their stem vowel (strong verbs) or have other irregular forms.
